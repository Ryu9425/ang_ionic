Ionic Framework

The most powerful HTML5 mobile development framework. Optimized for building great hybrid native apps.

Download v0.5 Explore examples

Fast

Ionic is fast. Native DOM interactions and no reliance on jQuery means Ionic apps are as fast as they can be. No markup enhancement means what you write is what you get.

Forward-thinking

We don't support old devices. Ionic focuses on the newest devices that have majority market share. No hacks for old-devices here.

Angular Friendly

Ionic works with great with your choice of framework (or none at all). For Angularjs developers, we've added special features to make Ionic and Angular a match made in heaven.
